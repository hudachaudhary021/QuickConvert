QuickConvert only processes the files you choose to upload for conversion. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect personal information beyond what you voluntarily submit through our contact form (name, email address, and message).

Files you upload are used solely to perform the requested conversion. Uploaded files and their converted output are automatically deleted from our servers shortly after your download completes, and in any case no later than 30 minutes after upload.

We apply reasonable technical safeguards to protect uploaded content during the brief period it exists on our servers, including file type validation and automatic deletion routines.
